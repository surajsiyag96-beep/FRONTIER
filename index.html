Harish Hudda

Chairman & CEO

Visionary Leadership for a Changing World

Under the strategic direction of Chairman Harish Hudda, Frontier has evolved from a regional mining operation into a global titan of natural resources. His philosophy centers on operational excellence, rigorous safety standards, and long-term economic stability.

"At Frontier, we understand that energy and materials are the bedrock of civilization. Our duty is to extract responsibly, process efficiently, and deliver relentlessly to ensure the world never stops moving forward."

Mr. Hudda brings over 30 years of industrial expertise, guiding Frontier through complex global market cycles. His current mandate focuses heavily on modernizing mining infrastructure, integrating AI in resource logistics, and mapping the company's sustainable transition pathways for the next decade.
